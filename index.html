About

NYC Restaurant Week FAQ

What is NYC Restaurant Week?
NYC Restaurant Week is a semiannual promotional program that celebrates fine dining at an accessible price. Restaurant Week participating restaurants offer affordable prix-fixe menus for lunch (two courses) and/or dinner (three courses).

Where should I make NYC Restaurant Week Winter 2020 table reservations?
Find and reserve the ​best NYC Restaurant Week Winter 2020 tables​ ​here.

Hashtag?
Check #nycrestaurantweek on Instagram to see what dishes and restaurants are trending. Pro tip: Search the geotags of participating restaurants to see what looks delicious before you book. When you dine, post your own photos and tag them with #nycrestaurantweek!

Where can I find the NYC Restaurant Week Dining Guide?
Booklets are available in participating restaurants and major hotels during the week prior to program launch. Unfortunately, we are unable to mail booklets to individuals.
